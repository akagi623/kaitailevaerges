# 問三⑴ キャラクター「ソラ」生成用プロンプト

画像生成AIに以下のYAMLブロックを貼り付けて、メインビジュアルを作成してください。

```yaml
target_image:
  subject: "Sora, a beautiful dragon girl hero"
  art_style: "High-end mobile game official art, Genshin Impact style, rich digital painting, vibrant colors"
  composition: "Cinematic wide-angle, full body or knee-up shot, looking at the viewer"

character_design:
  name: "Sora"
  features:
    - "Daughter of a Dragon: Delicate blue crystal-like horns on head"
    - "Wings: Massive, elegant dragon wings with sky-blue and translucent membranes"
    - "Clothing: White silk dress with intricate sky-blue fabric layers and ornate gold embroidery"
    - "Weapon: A ethereal bow made of glowing light particles and cyan crystals"
  color_scheme: "Dominant Sky-blue #87CEEB and Radiant Gold #FFD700"

environment:
  location: "A majestic floating city in the clouds (空中都市)"
  architecture: "White marble towers, golden domes, lush hanging gardens"
  atmosphere: "High altitude transparency, bright daylight, sunburst effect, fluffy white clouds below"

lighting_and_technical:
  lighting: "Cinematic backlighting, rim light on hair and wings, soft subsurface scattering for skin"
  details: "Hyper-detailed textures, metallic gold shine, glowing bow effects, volumetric lighting"
  quality: "Masterpiece, official art, 8k resolution, extreme high quality"

negative_prompt:
  - "Low quality, blurry, distorted face, extra limbs, messy lines, dark colors, realistic photo style"
```
